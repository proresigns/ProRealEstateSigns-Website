About Pro Real Estate Signs

Building The Region's Real Estate Sign Infrastructure.

Pro Real Estate Signs was created to modernize real estate sign management through professional field operations, organized inventory, in-house manufacturing, and technology-driven systems. We believe every brokerage deserves a dependable infrastructure partner that simplifies every step from listing to closing.

Industry Involvement

Committed to the Real Estate Industry.

Pro Real Estate Signs is proud to be an Affiliate Member of the Northwest Indiana REALTORS® Association (NIRA). We believe in supporting the real estate professionals we serve by investing in long-term relationships, industry involvement, and continuously improving the infrastructure that powers modern real estate sign management.

Why We Exist

Real Estate Sign Service Needed Better Infrastructure.

For years, brokerages managed sign installations through phone calls, text messages, spreadsheets, and disconnected processes. We saw an opportunity to build something better. Pro Real Estate Signs brings together professional field service, centralized order management, live visibility, inventory tracking, and in-house manufacturing into one organized system designed specifically for today's real estate industry.
