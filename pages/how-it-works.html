Simple, Organized Onboarding

How Your Account Gets Up and Running.

We handle the setup, inventory collection, asset tagging, and system organization so you can focus on your listings.

01

Request a New Account

Submit the online account request with your contact and brokerage information.

02

We Review Your Request

Our team reviews your information, confirms your service needs, and prepares your account.

03

Receive Your Invitation

Once approved, we add you to the system and email an invitation so you can create your login and access the dashboard.

04

Schedule Inventory Pickup

We coordinate a convenient day to collect your existing sign panels, riders, and other customer-owned inventory.

05

Asset Tagging & Tracking

Each inventory item receives an asset tag with a unique serial number, creating organized item-level tracking inside your account.

06

Order Installations

Submit installation and service requests through your dashboard. Need an installation before all inventory has been processed? We can accommodate that too.

Streamlined Office Setup

Enterprise & Brokerage Onboarding

Built for offices of every size.

Whether you're onboarding a single office or a multi-office brokerage, Pro Real Estate Signs makes the transition simple. Our streamlined onboarding process gets your team up and running quickly while eliminating hours of manual account setup.

Streamlined onboarding includes:

  • Bulk account creation from your current agent roster
  • Current active listings added to your dashboard
  • Fast setup for single offices, regional offices, and enterprise brokerages
  • Dedicated onboarding support from start to finish

Just provide us with your current agent roster and a list of your active listings. We'll take care of the rest.
